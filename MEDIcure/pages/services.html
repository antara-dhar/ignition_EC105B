Medical Services - Our Hospital

Cardiology

Our hospital offers comprehensive cardiology services including diagnosis, treatment, and management of heart conditions.

Orthopedics

We provide specialized orthopedic care for conditions affecting bones, joints, ligaments, and muscles, ensuring optimal recovery and mobility.

Neurology

Our neurology department offers expert diagnosis and treatment for disorders of the nervous system, ensuring personalized care and support for our patients.

Oncology

Our oncology team is dedicated to providing advanced cancer care, including diagnosis, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and supportive care services.

Pediatrics

We offer comprehensive pediatric care for children of all ages, focusing on preventive care, early intervention, and specialized treatment when needed.
